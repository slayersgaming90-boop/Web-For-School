AGNI

Agni is one of the most important Vedic Gods. He is the God of fire and acceptor of sacrifices. This tatva is associated with qualities of energy, assertiveness and passion. Its main characteristics are Shabda, Sparsha and Roopa (form)

PRITHVI

Prithvi is Earth and Hindu mother goddess. This tatva is the essense of element earth which is associated with Budha or Mercury who represents communication, business, mathematics and other practical matters. Its main characteristics are Shabda, Sparsha, Roopa, Rasa and Guna (quality).

JAL
