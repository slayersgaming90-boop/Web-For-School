Hybrid Teaching System

The Avenue Public School follows a hybrid system of online & offline teaching.

An online-offline hybrid learning system is a type of education delivery system that combines both online and offline learning methods. This approach provides students with the flexibility to study at their own pace and at a time that is convenient for them, while also giving them the opportunity to interact with teachers and classmates in person.

In this system, students are provided with online resources such as videos, lectures, quizzes, and assignments that they can access remotely. This allows students to learn at their own pace and from anywhere with an internet connection. Teachers use google classroom to monitor student progress and provide feedback in real-time.

On the other hand, students also attend physical classes at the school on a regular basis. Teachers use these classes to provide adequate support for any queries or instructions they conduct discussions, and engage in group activities.

Hybrid learning allows student to learn at their own pace and at a time that is convenient for them, while still providing opportunities for interaction with teachers and classmates. Hybrid learning can also help in bridging the gap between parents and teachers.

Overall, the online-offline hybrid learning system provides students with the best of both worlds. However, it requires careful planning and implementation to ensure that students receive a high-quality education that meets their learning needs.
