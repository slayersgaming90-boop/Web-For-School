School Rules & Code of Conduct

Parents are requested to co-operate with the school authorities to make their child a good and responsible citizen of the nation.

  1. School will not be responsible for personal belongings. Students should not bring valuable and costly items like watches, jewellery and mobile phones etc.
  2. All students must wear proper school uniform with special attention to personal hygiene and should keep their nails trimmed.
  3. School Diary is compulsory and must be brought to school every day.
  4. Punctuality is the key to success- Student must attend the school on all working days and try to achieve 100% attendance award. No students will be allowed to go home during the school hours. As such no half day leave / short leave can be granted. (Except in case of emergency relating to life and property)
  5. Half day leaves is allowed only in case of emergency relating to life/death or property.
  6. All students should be habitually clean and neatly dressed.
  7. Students are not allowed to meet visitors during school hours.
  8. Overall 75% attendance is compulsory during the academic year. No student will be absent from school without obtaining prior sanction from the Principal. A leave application duly signed by the parent must be submitted to the class teacher in advance.
  9. C.B.S.E. guidelines will be followed strictly in all the classes. A student with less than 75% attendance will have to provide a written explanation or he/she will be debarred from appearing in all Examinations (VI to XII).
