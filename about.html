About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence

Welcome to The Avenue Public School

The Avenue Public School (T.A.P.S.) is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education. As a Senior Secondary, Co-educational school affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E.), we strive to nurture holistic and independent learners.

Our vision is to empower students with knowledge, moral values, and global perspectives necessary to succeed in a rapidly changing world. By combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies, we offer a comprehensive educational experience.

Our Mission

  • Fostering critical thinking and creativity.
  • Encouraging a culture of lifelong learning.
  • Building character through strong ethical foundations.
  • Providing a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment.
